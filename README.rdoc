How narcissistic are you? This app will tell you exactly that. 

We use our own custom language-recognition algorithm to analyze your (or anyone else's) Twitter account to rate you on a narcissism scale, from Dalai Lama all the way to Kim Kardashian and Kanye West.

Created by Anna Ershova and Mitchell Hart at the Flatiron School, 2015.
